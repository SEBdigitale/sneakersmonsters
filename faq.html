FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Clear answers before you make your request.

Sneakers Monsters™
Do you sell authentic sneakers?

The goal is to source legitimate requested sneaker options. Buyers should still verify details, photos, condition, source, and expectations before purchase.

Can I request specific brands?

Yes. Use the request form and select the main brand from the dropdown, then add the exact model or colorway in the model field.

Can I request specific sizes?

Yes. Select the size category and type exact sizes in the notes field. Size clarity helps avoid wasted time.

Do you offer bulk packages?

Yes. The reseller packages are structured around 10, 20, and 30-pair requests, plus custom bulk quantities.

Why is the budget range a dropdown?

A preformatted budget range keeps the request organized and helps filter options that match the buyer’s reality.

Can beginners start reselling sneakers?

Beginners can learn the basics, test demand, and start carefully. They should not treat resale as guaranteed profit.

Do you guarantee profits?

No. Sneaker resale depends on demand, timing, condition, size, source, competition, and market movement.

How do I get pricing?

Go to Request Prices, complete the dropdowns and fields, and send the request with as much detail as possible.

How fast can I receive options?

Timing depends on request complexity, brand, model, size, quantity, and availability.

Are you affiliated with Nike, Jordan, Adidas, or other brands?

No. Sneakers Monsters™ is independent. Brand names are used only to identify products requested by customers.

What should I include in a reseller request?

Include the number of pairs, preferred brands, sizes, buyer demand, budget range, and how soon you need options.

Is the free book a newsletter?

No. It is positioned as a Sneakers Monsters™ field guide to help people understand the sneaker jungle before requesting or reselling.
