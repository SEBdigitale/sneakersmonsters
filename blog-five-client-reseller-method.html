Before thinking about bigger packages, test whether people around you actually want sneakers enough to commit.

Find five real buyers

Ask five people what brands, models, sizes, and price ranges they are looking for. Do not assume. Write it down.

Build a clean request sheet

Track each client, size, model, budget, and timeline. This turns hype into structured demand.

Match the package to demand

A 10-pair request makes more sense when you can already see real interest. Larger packages should follow stronger demand, not ego.
