Availability factors

Why one request can be different from another.

Sneaker availability can shift based on model, colorway, size, quantity, source, and timing. Two people asking for the same brand can need completely different answers.

Model matters

A general brand request is a start, but model names and colorways make it stronger.

Size matters

Some sizes can be easier or harder to source depending on the pair.

Timing matters

Drops, restocks, market attention, and local demand can change availability fast.
